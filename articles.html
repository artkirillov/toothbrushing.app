Why Your Oral Health Matters for Your Whole Body

Taking care of your mouth is about much more than avoiding toothaches. It’s about reducing inflammation and infection that could impact the rest of your body. Daily habits like brushing, flossing, and regular dental visits are simple but powerful tools to protect your health.
